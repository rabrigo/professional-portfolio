About
Me

I am a creative person that enjoys making music, dancing, performing, and making videos. After working as a technician, I decided to follow through with an idea I had during the first year of COVID: pursue a career path in tech. There were several elements informing this endeavor. At the time, I was living 30 minutes away from Silicon Valley. Several of my friends worked in tech. I have asthma which makes remote work ideal.

My skills informed this as well. All of my work experience involved computer use. I was exposed to coding in high school when I took AP Computer Science and when I was dabbling in game engines. At my most recent job, I was already practicing behaviors fundamental to working in tech: practicing analytical thinking, troubleshooting solutions, collaborating across different teams, and adopting a solution driven mindset.
